How to use Book It

What is Book It?

Book It is a web application that can help you plan your sports. We provide a free service and we do not charge for any use of the service. All charges for our services belong to the community centers we serve.

How can I book a sport?

Booking a sport is simple and easy.

Step 1

Click/tap on the Log in button

Step 2

Enter username and password OR sign in with new account

Step 3

Click/tap on see more sessions

Step 4

Click/tap on Book It

Finished

Click/tap on Go To my Events for list of current bookings.
